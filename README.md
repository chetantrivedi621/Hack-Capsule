## 🧠 About the Project
Hackathons are fast-paced and chaotic — ideas evolve rapidly, teams collaborate intensely, and valuable context often gets lost across chats, docs, or memory.

**Hack Capsule** solves this by acting as a **digital memory capsule** for hackathon teams, enabling them to:
- Capture ideas at any stage
- Collaborate efficiently under time pressure
- Track progress and decisions
- Preserve learnings even after the hackathon ends

This project blends **product thinking with solid engineering**, making it ideal for hackathons, student teams, and innovation-driven environments.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement
- Hackathon ideas are scattered across multiple tools
- No centralized system to track ideation → execution
- Team coordination becomes difficult under tight deadlines
- Post-hackathon learnings are rarely documented

---

## ✅ Solution
Hack Capsule provides a **centralized platform** where teams can:
- Create, refine, and store ideas collaboratively
- Organize tasks and workflows
- Maintain a persistent record of decisions and progress
- Revisit their entire hackathon journey anytime

---

## ✨ Key Features
- 🧠 **Idea Management** – Capture, edit, and evolve ideas collaboratively
- 👥 **Team Collaboration** – Seamless teamwork in shared workspaces
- 📦 **Memory Capsule** – Preserve the complete hackathon lifecycle
- 📊 **Workflow Tracking** – Track tasks, milestones, and progress
- 🔐 **Secure Access** – Authentication and controlled access
- ⚡ **Scalable Architecture** – Supports multiple teams and events
